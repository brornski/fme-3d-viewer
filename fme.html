The Process

How It Works

From consultation to transformation in four steps.

01

Assessment

Comprehensive CBCT scan and airway analysis to determine candidacy and create your custom treatment plan.

02

Custom Fabrication

Your FME device is precision-engineered from medical-grade titanium, custom-fit to your unique palatal anatomy.

03

Placement

6-10 TADs (Temporary Anchorage Devices) are placed to provide pure bone-borne expansion without touching your teeth.

04

Active Expansion

1-3 months of active expansion followed by 6+ months of stabilization. Total treatment time: 20-24 months.

Is FME Right For You?

Ideal Candidates

The FME is designed for adults seeking non-surgical palate expansion.

Adults 18+

Mature skeletal structure required for bone-borne expansion.

Narrow Palate

Patients with constricted maxillary arch seeking expansion without surgery.

Breathing Issues

Those with nasal obstruction, snoring, or sleep apnea symptoms.

TMJ Sufferers

Patients with jaw tension, clicking, or chronic headaches from TMJ dysfunction.
