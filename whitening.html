Our Whitening Options

In-Office Whitening

The fastest option — up to 8 shades lighter in a single 60-minute visit. Concentrated whitening gel is applied and activated with a special light for dramatic, immediate results.

Custom Take-Home Trays

Custom-fitted whitening trays made from your dental impressions with professional-strength whitening gel. Wear at home for 30-60 minutes daily over 1-2 weeks for gradual, controlled whitening.

Combination Treatment

The best of both worlds — start with in-office whitening for immediate results, then maintain and enhance with custom take-home trays for long-lasting brightness.

Touch-Up Treatments

Periodic in-office or take-home touch-ups keep your smile bright over time. We provide whitening gel refills for your custom trays so you can maintain results at home.

Why Professional Whitening?

Dramatically Better Results

Professional whitening uses higher concentration agents than over-the-counter products, delivering results up to 8 shades lighter. Drugstore strips typically achieve only 1-2 shades of improvement.

Safe & Supervised

Treatment is supervised by a dentist who customizes the process for your teeth and sensitivity level. Gum protection is applied to prevent irritation — something not possible with generic strips.

Even, Consistent Coverage

Custom trays and professional application ensure even whitening across all teeth — no streaks, missed spots, or uneven results common with over-the-counter products.

Fast Results

In-office whitening delivers results in a single visit. Take-home strips require weeks of daily use for modest improvements. Professional whitening respects your time.

Professional vs. Over-the-Counter Whitening

Concentration

Professional products contain 25-40% hydrogen peroxide or equivalent. Over-the-counter strips contain only 3-10%. Higher concentration means significantly better results in less time.

Custom Fit

Professional trays are made from your dental impressions for a perfect seal. Generic strips and trays don't conform to your teeth, leading to uneven whitening and gel contact with gums.

Safety

Professional whitening includes gum barriers, sensitivity management, and dental supervision. Over-the-counter products can cause gum burns and enamel damage if misused.

Longevity

Professional whitening results last 6 months to 2 years with proper care. Over-the-counter results fade much faster and require more frequent reapplication.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does teeth whitening cost?

In-office whitening costs $300-$800. Custom take-home trays cost $200-$400. Combination packages are available for the best value and results.

How long do results last?

Results last 6 months to 2 years depending on diet and habits. Avoiding coffee, wine, and tobacco extends results. Take-home trays allow easy touch-ups.

Is whitening safe for my teeth?

Yes. Professional whitening is safe and FDA-approved when supervised by a dentist. It does not damage enamel. Some temporary sensitivity is normal and resolves quickly.

Will my teeth be sensitive?

Some patients experience temporary sensitivity during or after treatment. It resolves within 24-48 hours. We use desensitizing agents and adjust intensity for sensitive patients.

Can whitening remove all stains?

Whitening is highly effective for extrinsic stains (coffee, wine, tobacco). Intrinsic stains (medication-related, fluorosis) may respond partially. Veneers are an alternative for stubborn discoloration.

In-office or take-home — which is better?

In-office delivers immediate results (1 visit). Take-home offers flexibility over 1-2 weeks. Combining both gives the best results with maintenance capability.
