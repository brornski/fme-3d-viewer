Our Cosmetic Services

Porcelain Veneers

Ultra-thin porcelain shells bonded to the front of teeth to transform their color, shape, size, and alignment. Veneers create a flawless, natural-looking smile with minimal tooth preparation.

Dental Crowns

Custom porcelain or ceramic caps that cover and protect damaged, weakened, or heavily filled teeth. Crowns restore full strength, function, and appearance — matched to your natural tooth color.

Dental Bridges

Replace one or more missing teeth with a bridge anchored to adjacent teeth or dental implants. Bridges restore your smile, bite, and prevent remaining teeth from shifting.

Inlays & Onlays

Custom-crafted restorations that repair moderate tooth damage — more conservative than crowns but stronger than fillings. Made from porcelain or composite for a seamless, durable repair.

Why Choose Cosmetic Dentistry?

Natural-Looking Results

Modern porcelain and ceramic materials mimic the translucency, color, and texture of natural teeth. Our restorations are virtually indistinguishable from your real teeth.

Restore Function & Strength

Cosmetic restorations don't just look good — crowns, bridges, and onlays restore full chewing function and protect damaged teeth from further breakdown.

Long-Lasting Durability

Porcelain crowns and veneers last 10-25 years with proper care. High-quality materials and precise fabrication ensure your investment stands the test of time.

Boost Confidence

A beautiful smile impacts every interaction — professionally and personally. Cosmetic dentistry eliminates self-consciousness about chipped, stained, or misshapen teeth.

The Cosmetic Process

Consultation & Design

We discuss your smile goals, examine your teeth, and present treatment options. Digital imaging can preview your expected results before any work begins.

Tooth Preparation

Minimal tooth structure is removed to accommodate the restoration. Digital or physical impressions are taken and sent to the dental lab for custom fabrication.

Custom Fabrication

Your restoration is handcrafted from premium materials, precisely matched to your natural tooth color and shape. A temporary restoration protects your tooth during fabrication.

Placement & Bonding

The final restoration is checked for fit, color, and bite, then permanently bonded or cemented. You leave with a beautiful, functional restoration that blends seamlessly.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much do veneers cost?

Porcelain veneers cost $900-$2,500 per tooth. Composite veneers cost $250-$1,500 per tooth. Cost depends on material and number of veneers. We offer financing options.

How long do crowns last?

Porcelain crowns typically last 10-15 years, with many lasting 25+ years with proper care. Avoiding hard foods and wearing a night guard (if you grind) extends longevity.

Crown vs. veneer — what's the difference?

Crowns cover the entire tooth for strength. Veneers cover only the front surface for cosmetic improvement. Crowns are for damaged teeth; veneers are for appearance changes.

Does insurance cover cosmetic work?

Insurance typically covers medically necessary restorations (crowns for damaged teeth, bridges for missing teeth). Elective cosmetic work may not be covered. We help maximize your benefits.

How many visits does it take?

Most restorations require 2 visits over 2-3 weeks — preparation and impressions at visit 1, final placement at visit 2. Some same-day crowns available.

Are veneers permanent?

Veneers require removing a thin layer of enamel, making them a permanent commitment. However, the veneers themselves last 10-20 years and can be replaced when needed.
