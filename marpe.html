Miniscrew-Assisted Rapid Palatal Expansion — custom-fitted skeletal expansion for adults and teens.

Custom-Fitted

Custom MARPE Expander

A custom MARPE expander is precision-engineered to your unique palatal anatomy using digital impressions and CBCT imaging. Four titanium mini-implants (TADs) anchor the device directly to bone, delivering true skeletal expansion with minimal dental side effects — no surgery required.

How Custom MARPE Works

Digital Planning

CBCT scans and digital impressions map your palatal bone thickness and anatomy. Your custom MARPE is designed for optimal fit and force distribution.

Custom Fabrication

Your MARPE device is precision-manufactured to your exact measurements, ensuring a perfect fit that maximizes skeletal expansion and minimizes dental side effects.

TAD Placement & Activation

Four titanium mini-implants are placed under local anesthesia in approximately 15 minutes. Daily activation with a key gradually expands the palatal suture over several weeks.

Stabilization & Finishing

After reaching target expansion, the MARPE remains in place for 9-12 months while new bone fills the expanded suture. Orthodontic finishing aligns teeth into the wider arch.

Benefits of Custom MARPE

Precision Custom Fit

Unlike generic expanders, a custom MARPE is designed from your 3D anatomy. This ensures optimal TAD positioning, better force distribution, and fewer complications.

Non-Surgical Expansion

MARPE eliminates the need for SARPE surgery. No general anesthesia, no surgical bone cuts, and recovery takes just 1-2 days instead of weeks.

Improved Nasal Breathing

Expanding the maxilla widens the nasal floor, increasing nasal airway volume. Patients often report significant relief from mouth breathing, snoring, and obstructive sleep apnea symptoms.

Minimal Dental Side Effects

Bone-borne TAD anchorage applies forces directly to the palatal bone rather than teeth. This produces true skeletal expansion with less tooth tipping than traditional expanders.

Who Is a Candidate for MARPE?

Adults with Narrow Palates

Adults whose palatal sutures have fused (typically after age 16-18) are ideal MARPE candidates. Traditional expanders cannot achieve skeletal expansion in adults.

Crowding & Crossbites

Patients with dental crowding, posterior crossbites, or arch width discrepancies caused by a constricted maxilla benefit from skeletal-level widening.

Airway & Breathing Issues

Patients with chronic mouth breathing, nasal obstruction, snoring, or obstructive sleep apnea related to a narrow maxilla and restricted nasal airway.

Surgery-Averse Patients

Patients who want to avoid SARPE surgery or who are not candidates for surgical intervention can achieve comparable skeletal expansion with custom MARPE.

MARPE vs. Other Options

Custom MARPE vs. SARPE

SARPE requires surgery under general anesthesia with longer recovery. MARPE achieves similar skeletal expansion non-surgically with greater parallel expansion and a V-shaped opening pattern more similar to natural growth.

Custom MARPE vs. RPE

Traditional RPE (Rapid Palatal Expansion) relies on teeth for anchorage and only works in children. MARPE uses bone-borne TADs for true skeletal expansion in adults.

Custom MARPE vs. MSE

Both are TAD-anchored skeletal expanders. A custom MARPE is fabricated to your exact anatomy for optimal fit, while standard MSE devices use a more universal design.

Custom MARPE vs. FME

The Facegenics Midface Expander (FME) is Team Dental's flagship device using 6-10 TADs for maximum stability. Your doctor will recommend the best expander for your case.
